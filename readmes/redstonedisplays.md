# Redstone Displays

Redstone-powered display indicators — custom heads that change appearance based on the signal
strength they receive (0-15).

See [the catalog of indicators](https://def9a2a4.github.io/defCoreLib-docs/index.html?q=indicator).

[Download on Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/plugin/redstonedisplays)

![Indicators being used to show signal strength from a lectern.](https://def9a2a4.github.io/defCoreLib-docs/readmes/assets/rsd/indicators-lectern.png)

## Features

- Three styles — **Digital**, **Binary**, and **Panel** — each with 16 textures for power 0-15.
- Reads redstone power through walls (2 blocks behind for wall-mounted, 2 below for floor-mounted).
- Recipes unlock when you enter the Nether. Crafted from stone, redstone, quartz, and amethyst.
- Purely server-side Paper plugin — no mods or resource packs required.

![All 3 variants of indicators at all 16 power levels](https://def9a2a4.github.io/defCoreLib-docs/readmes/assets/rsd/indicators-wall.png)

## Requires

[DefCoreLib](https://github.com/def9a2a4/defCoreLib/blob/main/docs/readmes/defCoreLib.md).

The three indicator blocks live in DefCoreLib; this plugin enables their crafting recipes. Without
it the blocks still exist (obtainable via `/defcorelib give redstonedisplays:digital_indicator`),
they just aren't craftable.
